### Open ___
This week, we were all tasked with researching open source media/movements that were not solely surrounded by software, and we came to some interesting findings.
I talked about OpenStax in class; a free access college textbook movement created by faculty at Rice University to increase accessibility to necessary learning
materials without disenfranchising students that have economic struggles. I talked about this in class so I won't go too far into detail here. However, some of my
peers ended up discussing some really interesting ones, like OpenCola, which was an open source soda recipe, and Stellarium, which democratizes astronomy via user 
submitted information. It makes me happy to know that the philosophies of open source technology hold up outside of the tech space, and still make the world better
for some people in some ways beyond just tech.
