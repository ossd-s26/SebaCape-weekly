### The Final Stretch
After this post, one week of classes remain, and everyone in our class will begin to give their presentations on the work they have done on their project,
alongside key takeaways they have developed, and things they have learned. The first group to present is lo and behold: Keycloak. Despite this, I don't really
feel that nervous at all. My group and I have been doing the work all the way up until this point, and now we are simply having the opportunity to share all that
we have been developing over time. Just earlier today, we had our first practice runs of our presentation in person, and I am pretty confident that come tomorrow
we will do a great job in showcasing all of the work we have done that is both informative and entertaining. The theming is pretty nice too in my opinion. There 
isn't much else to say until the presentation, so I will let the work we have been doing over the past week preparing everything speak for itself.
